ERP for postal company

Automation of business processes of a postal company

The challenge

The main task was to understand all the processes of the company, identify strengths and weaknesses (bottle necks), as well as to automate all the processes. The specifics of the company's work implied the presence of many partner companies at each stage of the parcel's journey, as well as a large number of procedures related to the exchange of information with the customs systems of different countries. Despite the large number of such systems on the market, the specifics of working with Ukraine led to the client's decision to make the system, sharpened exclusively for the needs of his company. An important aspect of the system is its internationality, as well as the ability to work comfortably in countries with slow Internet connections. For the company's management it was very important to get information about the main indicators of the company and through extraordinary situations in real time to have a complete picture of what is happening.

The solution

After a detailed study of all business processes, an ERP-system was developed, which features modularity and flexibility in terms of connecting different customs API and API of partner companies. The system was created taking into account the fact that the company's employees have different level of experience with PCs, speak different languages and are responsible for individual processes. All processes in the company are now unified and conducted through one system, regardless of the country, language and speed of the Internet. The system is also synchronized with the website and personal cabinet for the company's clients, which allows to automatically receive and place orders, notify clients about promotions, goods arrived and changes in the company's operations.

CORE FUNCTIONALITY

API request template engine

A large number of customs and partner APIs have essentially the same functions, but implemented slightly differently. We have identified the main functions, data and properties for such requests and created a universal template engine that allows you to create and correct API requests without the intervention of the developer.

Template engine for customs declarations and related documents

As with the API, documents have almost the same data, but different shapes. That's why we've created a module that allows you to load document templates of different shapes and pull the necessary data out of the system. Now managers do not need to prepare documents manually, as the system itself selects the desired form of the document and fills it with data on parcels.

Flexible multilinguality

The "Languages" module allows you to translate all the entities and names in the system into the desired language, and also takes into account the ability to transform the interface from right to left and vice versa. Now branch managers can create their own localization and name the entities in the most convenient way for them.

Flexible notification system

Working with customer alerts is one of the important parts of the system, as the main value of the company: everywhere to do so that the customer was calm for his cargo. That's why we have developed a flexible client notification module that works with major messengers and sms. The main feature of the system is the ability to configure automatic alerts at different stages of the parcel, as well as to form different user groups. This functionality allows you to personalize alerts and maintain excellent relations with customers.

Flexible system of access rights, branches and partner companies

With time, business processes are transformed, branches are added, and partner companies have different terms of cooperation. In this regard, we have created a fully customizable system of access rights and user roles, allowing management to provide employees and partners with only the system features they need.

Customizable statistics in real time

A postal company is a complex process business that can only be effectively controlled by having all the key performance indicators in real time. The CEO of the company can learn everything that is happening in the company at any time from any device, as well as compare the current figures with the past.
