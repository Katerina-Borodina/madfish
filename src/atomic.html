Atomic swap

Automatic exchange of ERC20 token on Mainnet and vice versa without intermediaries.

The challenge

Cryptocurrency startup, who collected ICO in ERC20 tokens, issued his coin. It took the opportunity to exchange ERC20 for the equivalent in the main currency conveniently, reliably and without intermediaries. A very important part of the task was the reliability and security of the system, as it was about investors' money.

The solution

After the research, a web service was developed, which allows for a few clicks to make an exchange. The interface described all the steps and provided instructions so that the user does not worry about their tokens during the exchange. The solution was also incorporated into the cryptocurrency wallet (link to the case), for even more convenient use.
