ERP for Ukraininan government company

System for accounting, automation and acceleration of order processing in the government project company

The challenge

We had to understand all the processes of the company, identify strengths and weaknesses (bottle necks), as well as automate all the processes, while making them more transparent and efficient. It was important to merge all the departments into one coherent structure that could react quickly to changes. And to give management the opportunity to get all the necessary information in real time, which would allow them to make decisions faster and more accurately. Also an important part of the task was to make a system that could be easily understood by the user of any level of PC use. An additional complication was the fact that the company had many branches in different cities of the country, which had to constantly coordinate their activities with the central office.

The solution

After a detailed study of all processes and their bottlenecks, the peculiarities of each employee of the company, as well as detailed collection of requirements from management, an ERP-system was developed, covering all tasks related to the company's operation, as well as synchronization with the accounting system and the resource for data exchange with customers. Employees of the company refused from all additional software, except for professional engineering programs. All communication with customers and employees, document flow, reporting, file storage, analytics, postal services, as well as order value calculations were combined into one system and became maximally optimized for the activity of a particular company. When using the new system - the speed of order processing increased by 17%, and was almost completely eliminated one of the departments of the company, engaged in the execution of standard documents.

CORE FUNCTIONALITY

Synchronization of finance with the accounting system in real time

The company has quite complex financial processes and a whole staff of accountants who serve it all. Before the system was created, they had to manually collect data from other departments and combine it with bank payments. Now the system enters into the system all potential payments in real time and forms the most part of the documents connected with their carrying out.

Built-in document template engine and automatic document generation

The presence of a large number of orders clearly signalled the need to automate the formation of contracts and related documents. Now, depending on many factors, the system selects the most appropriate type of contract, substitutes the necessary data about the order and the client and generates all the documentation, which can be easily downloaded or immediately sent to the customer for consideration.

Built-in archive with order documentation

A huge amount of accompanying legal and engineering documentation has prompted us to make an internal storage for each order with a simple and convenient system of work with the main file formats. Now employees never lose documents, they can easily and quickly share, view and edit them.

Service for fast documentation exchange with customers

In order to avoid clutter in email correspondence with clients and the use of third-party file sharing services, a fast file exchange service between the client and the company has been developed. It is synchronized with the internal file sharing service and allows you to quickly exchange information with customers and other designers without using third-party systems.

Logging and performance measurement system for employees

Having a huge number of orders and related documentation you can make a mistake, which can be difficult to track. Every action in the system is recorded so that the management can foresee the wrong actions or track who made the mistake. Also, logging of all actions allows you to track the efficiency of employees and time spent on work.

Automatic order value calculation system

The company had employees who were solely responsible for calculating the value of each order. The cost depends on a large number of parameters specified in the engineering documentation, the speed of work, the type of company and many others. We have managed to transfer this algorithm into the code and automate this process, which later made it unnecessary to have evaluators within the company.

Flexible system of access rights for users and branches

We realized that over time, the roles of employees and many processes in the company will be transformed. So we created a flexible system of roles and access rights that allows the manager to create unique access rights, if required.
